Welcome to CodingCareer.com

Hi, my name is Emily Raper and I am the founder of CodingCareer.com, an online coding and web development education platform that focuses on the key coding skills needed to have a successful career in the industry. This platform is a unique educational innovation the offers high quality education without the time commitment and expense of traditional degree programs. The business also focuses on individuals preparing to enter the workforce and caters its content towards the desires of employers, not academics. CodingCareer.com gives the power and freedom back to the students and improves the computer science and web development industry by equipping those who are next to enter it.

Mission

CodingCareer.com is about improving the web development and programming industry, through equipping its future employees with an innovative and efficient education solution.

Today's Web Development Industry

The future of this industry is promoting various forms of education in an area of growing need. Computer science and programming positions are increasing in quantity and demand, and if there were a more efficient and less expensive alternative to four-year computer science degree programs, more of these positions could be filled and more people would have the skills to be hired for such positions. Programs like bootcamps and already-existing online tutorial sites and videos are helping this process along and I believe that these new education methods within the development industry can rub off on other industries as well, providing more opportunities to those that are unable to afford college or make the time for it.
